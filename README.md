# Privacy Policy - Chicken-Identifier
Last Updated: [2026/01/07]

Chicken-Identifier is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to Chicken-Identifier and its various versions (collectively referred to as “we”, “us”, or “our”) and forms part of our Terms of Use.
By accessing or using the Application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy and consent to the practices described herein.

# 2. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information when you use the Application:
- Subscription and payment data (processed securely via the Apple App Store)
- Device and usage information, such as device model, operating system version, IP address, app interactions, and crash logs
- Uploaded images of chickens submitted by users for AI-powered breed identification and analysis (temporarily processed)
- We do not collect personal identity information unless explicitly provided by the user (e.g., support emails).

# 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
- Provide and improve AI-powered chicken breed identification features
- Process uploaded images and generate analysis results
- Manage subscriptions and in-app purchases
- Monitor app performance, stability, and usage trends
- Improve user experience and application functionality
- Respond to support requests and communicate important updates

# 4. Image Data & AI Processing
- Uploaded images are processed temporarily on secure servers solely for chicken identification purposes.
- Images are automatically deleted within 1 hour after processing.
- Uploaded images are not used for training AI models, resale, or any secondary purpose.
- We do not store image metadata beyond what is required for processing.
- Images are handled in compliance with applicable data protection standards.

# 4A. Face Data & Biometric Information
- The Application is designed to identify chickens only and does not target humans.
- Chicken-Identifier does not collect, analyze, store, or infer any facial data, biometric identifiers, or human identity information.
- If an uploaded image incidentally contains a human face, it is ignored by the system and not analyzed.
- No facial recognition, face mapping, biometric analysis, or identity verification is performed.
- The Application does not create or retain any data capable of identifying an individual.
